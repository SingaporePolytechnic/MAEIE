THE SMART INDUSTRY READINESS INDEX

The Smart Industry Readiness Index (SIRI) was created by the Singapore Economic Development Board (EDB) to help manufacturers in their transformation journey.

In this index, there are 3 fundamental building blocks of i4.0: Technology, Process, and Organisation. Underpinning them are 8 key pillars which represent the important aspects for manufactures to focus on. Finally, all 3 building blocks and 8 pillars are map onto 16 dimensions, which are the areas to evaluate the current i4.0 readiness of their facilities.
